Program Overview

This 5-day workout program focuses on machine-based exercises to build strength while targeting lower belly fat. The plan is designed for Shahda's specific schedule (Sunday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday, Saturday) and includes targeted cardio and core work to support overall fitness goals.

Training Guidelines

Rest & Recovery

  • Aim for 6.5-7 hours of sleep each night
  • Take rest days seriously to avoid injury
  • Maintain proper hydration
  • Listen to your body and adjust as needed

Key Recommendations

  • Maintain consistency with 4-5 workouts per week
  • Focus on proper form over lifting heavy weights
  • Progress gradually by increasing weight or reps
  • Combine with appropriate nutrition for best results
